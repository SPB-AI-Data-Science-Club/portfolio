Upcoming Event

Machine Learning Workshop
& Mini Datathon

A hands-on introduction to machine learning followed by a friendly team competition on a real dataset. Open to all students. No programming or AI experience is required; club members guide every team from start to finish.

Date & Time To be announced Check back soon or follow club announcements.
Location Stratford Preparatory Blackford Auditorium
Cost Free to attend Bring a laptop if you have one; teams share equipment.
Food Lunch & snacks provided Pizza, with vegetarian options available.
Prizes
1st Place $100
2nd Place $75
3rd Place $50

Additional smaller prizes will be awarded beyond the top three teams.

How the Day Works
1

Learn

A guided session walks every participant through building and training a first machine learning model in Python, from loading a dataset to reading the results. All materials are provided.

2

Build

Teams apply what they learned to the competition dataset, with club members circulating to help debug, explain concepts, and suggest improvements.

3

Compete

Each team submits its model for scoring on a held-out test set. Standings are shown live, and winners are announced at the end of the event.

Good to Know
  • Open to all Stratford Preparatory Blackford students, middle and high school.
  • Teams are formed at the event, so you can come alone or with friends.
  • Models train on the club's own GPU servers, the same infrastructure behind the nine live club projects.
  • Questions are welcome; contact any club officer listed on the About page.
